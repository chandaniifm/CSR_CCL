Why This Matters

India has the world's highest TB burden. Jharkhand's tribal districts are among the most affected. Malnutrition ‚Äî endemic in CCL's operational areas ‚Äî is a leading cause of TB treatment failure. Nutrition intervention is clinically proven to improve cure rates by up to 40%.
